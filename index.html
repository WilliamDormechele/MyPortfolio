About Me

I am a results-driven data engineer and developer, passionate about building secure, scalable systems that make a genuine impact. With over five years of experience across health informatics, data science, and data engineering, I specialise in transforming complex requirements into elegant, reliable solutions—whether for enterprise, or public health environments.

My journey has taken me from managing big health data platforms in Ghana to completing my MSc in Information Technology (Distinction) at Bournemouth University. Along the way, I have engineered automated data pipelines, developed web applications for SOPs and ethics management, and integrated APIs for real-time notifications and analytics. My technical toolkit includes Python, JavaScript, SQL, PhP, R, Power BI, Tableau, and modern web technologies, but I am just as comfortable collaborating with multidisciplinary teams or translating technical concepts for non-specialists.

I thrive in dynamic, mission-driven settings where high standards, curiosity, and community matter. Currently, I am keen to contribute my skills and enthusiasm to projects at the intersection of production engineering, automation, and cloud infrastructure. I welcome opportunities to learn, collaborate, and help shape systems that deliver lasting value.

Standard Operating Procedure Management App

Overview:
A secure, role-based web platform for managing SOPs, approvals, version history, and user acknowledgment within research environments.

  • Role-based access: Admin, HOD, Officer, Guest, etc.
  • Upload and approve SOPs with version control
  • Assign SOPs to specific users and track acknowledgments
  • Amendment management with two-stage review and closure
  • Dashboard with SOP stats and user task tracking
  • Audit trail and Excel export of logs

Built with:
Flask (Python), SQLite, Jinja2, Bootstrap 5, SQLAlchemy, SweetAlert, HTML/CSS

REDCap + SMS Integration

Overview:
A backend integration that connects REDCap survey submissions with the Hubtel SMS API to automate real-time SMS notifications for research participants.

  • Connects REDCap via API to Hubtel SMS platform
  • Triggers SMS alerts when form is submitted or updated
  • Flexible message templates with phone field mapping
  • Logs all messages sent for monitoring

Building with:
Python, REDCap API, Hubtel API, JSON, Flask

Research Ethics Portal

Overview:
A full-featured IRB portal for managing research protocol submissions, reviewer workflows, board approvals, and ethical clearance reporting.

  • Submit, review, and approve research protocols
  • Reviewer dashboards with deadlines and PDF export
  • Admin tools for comment collation and resolution tracking
  • Custom audit trail for decisions, edits, and reminders
  • Email notifications for re-reviews and submissions

Building with:
Flask, SQLite, SQLAlchemy, Jinja2, HTML/CSS, Bootstrap 5
